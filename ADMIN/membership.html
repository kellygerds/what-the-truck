LEVEL OF MEMBERSHIP
Level One

Silver Vendor ($300/year):
For the new truck owner who is trying to save money, but still looking to incorporate a rewards program into their app

-Badge shared for checking into the truck 5 times on social media
-Badge shared for most creative picture posted on social media that week
-Badge shared for being the 100th, 200th, etc follower of the truck



Level Two

Gold Vendor ($350/year):
For a seasoned truck owner looking to reward loyal customers with a free bite or snack from time to time to keep them coming back

-Badge shared for a free drink once visited 5 times
-Badge shared for a free side dish once visited 8 times
-Badge shared for a free app or dessert item once visited 10 times



Level Three

Platinum Vendor ($400/year):
For the food truck veteran, the owner who has been in business for years and is ready to really expand their customer base by offering free meals with loyalty

-Badge shared for free *key menu item* such as a sandwich, burger, wrap, etc once visited 10 times
-Badge shared for free meal (side, main dish and drink) once visited 15 times
-Badge shared for buy one get 2 free side dishes or add ons once visited 20 times
-Frequent Foodie badge-awarded after their 20th visit, 5% off every time they come to the truck or the choice to rollover and restart rewards
-*Bonus for Vendor* they will be placed at the top of lists of feeds that populate to users
